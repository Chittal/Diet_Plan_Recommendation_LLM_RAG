About Us

At NutriBuddy, we are dedicated to helping people achieve a healthier lifestyle by providing personalized diet plans powered by AI. We believe that the right nutrition is key to maintaining optimal health, and we strive to offer solutions tailored to the unique needs of each individual.

We are a passionate team of nutrition experts, health professionals, and data scientists working together to bring innovative and effective solutions to improve your well-being. With our AI-powered platform, we analyze your health conditions and provide customized diet recommendations to help you manage chronic diseases like heart disease, thyroid disorders, and diabetes.
